One World. One Breath
Origin

We observed that most organizations overlook the power of the individual, becoming detached from ordinary people. Coesse is dedicated to connecting every individual worldwide. Starting from simple actions—recycling a bottle or respecting others—is the true beginning of defending our planet and human rights.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To mobilize global volunteers to protect the planet and uphold and promote human rights.

Vision: To create a world where all life is respected and sustainable by connecting volunteers around the world!
