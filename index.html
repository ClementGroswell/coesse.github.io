Origin

Coesse was born from the observation that most organizations overlook the power of the individual. We believe that global change starts from the ordinary—respecting others and recycling a single bottle are the beginnings of defending human rights and our planet.

Our Commitment

Mission: To mobilize global volunteers to protect the planet and uphold and promote human rights.

Vision: To create a world where all life is respected and sustainable by connecting volunteers around the world!
