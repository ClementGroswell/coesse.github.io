COESSE

One World. One Breath.
Origin

We observed that most organizations overlook the power of the individual. Coesse is dedicated to connecting every individual worldwide. Starting from simple actions—recycling a bottle—is the beginning of defending our planet and human rights.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To mobilize global volunteers to protect the planet and uphold human rights.

Vision: To create a world where all life is respected by connecting volunteers around the world!
