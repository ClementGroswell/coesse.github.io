Our Birth

Coesse is a global volunteer organization dedicated to protecting the planet and defending human rights, driven by volunteers who join voluntarily to take action.

Coesse was founded because most environmental and human rights organizations often overlook the power of individuals worldwide, overemphasizing the official role of organizations and governments, while being trapped in huge, bloated systems that gradually lose the vitality and efficiency that international organizations should have.

For this reason, Coesse has launched a brand-new approach that emphasizes the relationship between individuals, the world, and the planet. Starting from the ordinary: recycling a plastic bottle, respecting others in daily life, and keeping kindness in heart — all these are the beginning of changing human rights and protecting the Earth.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To mobilize global volunteers to protect the planet, defend human rights, and foster a culture of life coexistence for present and future generations.

Vision

A future world where humanity and the planet coexist harmoniously, a world where all life and species are respected and protected, and where sustainable development is achieved.

Join Us

Every individual action counts. Join our global volunteer community to protect the planet and defend human rights, starting from today.
