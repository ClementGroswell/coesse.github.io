Mission & Vision

Mission | 官方使命宣言

To mobilize global volunteers to protect the planet, defend human rights, and foster a culture of life coexistence for present and future generations.

Vision | 官方愿景

A world where humanity and nature coexist in harmony, where every life is respected, and where collective action shapes a just and sustainable future.

一个人与自然和谐共存、每个生命都被尊重、通过集体行动创造公正可持续未来的世界。
