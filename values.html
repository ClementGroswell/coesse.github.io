Core Values

Coexistence — 共存

We believe that the Earth and all life are interconnected and should be protected and have rights.

Human Dignity — 人类尊严

Every person has inherent rights, worth, and the freedom to live safely and with respect.

Planetary Responsibility — 地球责任

We act as stewards of the planet for future generations.

Collective Action — 共同行动

Real change happens through every individual; it will change the world when people unite and participate across cultures and national borders!

Integrity & Transparency — 诚信与透明

We conduct our business with the principles of integrity, responsibility, transparency, and earning public trust.

Core Belief:

We believe the Earth is a living system where all life is interconnected, worthy of protection and fundamental rights.
